About dawak

Dawak app enables you to order your medications online. We will notify you through a push notification and SMS when your refill for chronic medication becomes due. Simply accept the home delivery option and select a convenient time slot for delivery. Our pharmacists will pack your medications in pill packs, ensuring easy organization and adherence.
